Welcome to SOCH College of Information Technology 


      SOCH College of Information Technology is a college located in Nepal
      that offers courses and programs related to information technology. 
      They provide education and training in various areas of IT such as 
      software development, networking, cybersecurity, database management,
      and more.The college is committed to providing quality education and
      practical training to prepare students for careers in the IT industry.
      They offer a range of degree and diploma programs, as well as various
      certification courses and training programs.

B.Sc.CSIT

B.Sc.CSIToffered by Institute of Science and Technology, Tribhuvan University, Nepal is a four years 8 semesters academic program.

This program ensures that graduates have a broad basic knowledge and

creative skills to conceive, design, build and exploit Information

Processing Systems. In addition to this, the program also stimulates

development of soft skills such as team work, effective communication,

technology management etc. These skills are acquired through the execution

of various projects, lab works, presentations, case studies and seminars

throughout the entire program.

It is a challenging and demanding program. Nevertheless it is also rewarding

and fun. Thus it is the right program for students who are passionate about

Technology and Communication.
